FIVE STAGES OF PUBERTY

Boy's Stages of Puberty

  1. No visible changes in the first stage
  2. Pubic hair starts to form
  3. Voice begins to change or "crack"; muscles get larger
  4. Acne may appear; armpit hair forms
  5. Facial hair comes in

Girl's Stages of Puberty

  1. No noticeable changes in the first stage
  2. Breast "buds" start to form; pubic hair starts to form
  3. Acne first appears; armpit hair forms; height increases faster
  4. First period arrives
  5. Reproductive organs and genitals are fully developed
